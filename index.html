About

I am a researcher at IBM Research and the Open University of Israel working at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and AI Evaluation. My research focuses on understanding and improving the robustness and reliability of large language models — including how they fail, how to evaluate them efficiently, and how to make them more robust.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Learning
  • AI Evaluation
  • Large Language Models
  • Robustness
  • Table Reasoning
